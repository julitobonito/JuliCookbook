## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 1/2 onion (sliced)
- 3 scallions (whites for cooking, greens for garnish)
- 400g well-fermented kimchi
- 50ml kimchi juice
- 500ml dashi stock (use vegetable dashi to keep it vegetarian)
- 250-500g tofu (depending on how "full" you want the stew to be)
- Salt to taste

### Sauce

- 2 tbsp gochujang
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp gochugaru flakes
- 2 tsp sugar
- 3 cloves garlic (grated)
- 1/2 inch knob ginger (grated)

## Instructions

1. Prep and slice the onion, scallions, and kimchi.
2. Combine all the sauce ingredients in a bowl.
3. In a pot, heat the neutral oil over medium heat.
4. Stir-fry the kimchi for a couple of minutes, then add the onions and scallion whites. Sauté for a few more minutes until the onions are slightly translucent.
5. Add the sauce, kimchi juice, and dashi stock. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
6. Add the tofu, cover with a lid, and simmer for a few more minutes. Season with salt to taste.
7. Garnish with the scallion greens and serve with rice.
