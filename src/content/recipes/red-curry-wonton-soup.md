## Ingredients

- 24 frozen chicken wontons (or dumplings)
- A bunch of Asian greens

### Red Curry Soup

- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 onion (finely chopped)
- 1 thumb piece ginger (grated)
- 2 garlic cloves (minced)
- 4 tbsp red curry paste (store-bought)
- 4 cups chicken stock
- 1 can coconut milk
- 1 1/2 tbsp caster sugar (or to taste)
- 1 tbsp fish sauce (or to taste)
- Lime juice, to taste

### Garnish

- Fresh herbs (spring onion, coriander, or Thai basil)
- Long red chilli (sliced)
- Fried shallots

## Instructions

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, ginger, and garlic, and cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring until fragrant and soft.
2. Add the red curry paste and cook for a further minute.
3. Mix in the chicken stock, coconut milk, caster sugar, and fish sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10 minutes. Add lime juice, taste, and season as desired.
4. Boil the wontons and Asian greens separately, then transfer to serving bowls.
5. Ladle over the hot soup and garnish with fresh herbs, chopped chilli, and fried shallots.
