## Ingredients

- 12 frozen wontons (pick to your liking, chicken, veggie, whatever floats your boat)
- A bunch of greens (Pak Choi, Broccoli)

### Red Curry Soup

- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 onion (finely chopped)
- 1 thumb piece ginger (grated)
- 2 garlic cloves (minced)
- 3 tbsp red curry paste (store-bought)
- 4 cups chicken stock (or vegetable stock)
- 1 can coconut milk
- 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp fish sauce (add more for more intense flavor)
- Lime juice, to taste

### Garnish

- Fresh herbs (spring onion, coriander, you name it)
- Long red chilli (sliced)
- Sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, ginger, and garlic, and cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring until fragrant and soft.
2. Add the red curry paste and cook for a further minute.
3. Mix in the chicken stock, coconut milk, sugar, and fish sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10 minutes. Add lime juice, taste, and season as desired.
4. Boil the wontons in the stock.
5. Prepare the greens separately, then either add on top of the soup or serve separately.
6. Ladle over the hot soup and garnish with fresh herbs, chopped chilli, and sesame seeds.
