## Ingredients

- 1 apple (chopped)
- 1/2 tbsp honey
- 80g porridge oats
- 80ml milk
- 80ml water
- Salt to taste
- 60g protein powder (optional)
- Cinnamon to taste
- Cardamom to taste

### Garnish

- Fresh fruit
- Yogurt or Skyr

## Instructions

1. Add 3/4 of the chopped apple into a pot. Keep the rest for garnish.
2. Add the honey and let simmer for a minute or two.
3. Mix in the porridge oats, milk, and water. Add protein powder, cinnamon, and cardamom as desired.
4. Let the porridge simmer for 3–4 minutes until it reaches your desired texture.
5. Serve in bowls and add the fresh fruit and a scoop of yogurt or Skyr.
